About Me

My name is Kevin Salgado Antonio. I have been very passionate about coding since elementary school. In 4th grade, I was given an assignment on a website called Code.org. When given the assignment, I quickly became really attached to it, finished the entire assignment in one day, and was really curious to learn more coding. As the years went by, I did different types of coding. I remember doing a project in middle school based on the game Ping Pong on an online website. As well as working on a group project with my classmates to learn how to program a robot and make it do things like move around, pick things up, and repeat certain actions with a given command. I'm really grateful to have been given the chance to experience coding at such a young age. It has really influenced my life and led me to where I am now. Before code became an aspiration, I was really into dinosaurs, like every other kid, and wanted to be a paleontologist. That dream quickly died after I was told that when I became a paleontologist, there would be no more fossils for me to dig up.

Besides school, something I am really into is Pokémon. I find Pokémon to be such a fun and wonderful thing. Pokémon is a universe where anyone can aspire to be who they want to be by following their goals and with the help from friends and companions. I have learn many things from watching the show, but a main message that sticks out is to never give up on your goals, and to give it your very best. I try to live by that idea and try to never give up no matter how hard it get.
